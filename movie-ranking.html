The Best Movies According to Dhana

My top 3 favorite movies of all-time

Tower

I love a good true crime documentary and this one was done beautifully.
The cartoon elements and real surviors sharing their stories set this doc apart from all others.

The School for Good and Evil

A fantasy film with epic plot twists. Based on a book and it includes one of my favorite actresses, Sofia Wylie.

Sky High

2005 superhero comedy. Love me a throwback Disney movie.
